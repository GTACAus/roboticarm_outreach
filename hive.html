DEFINE STAGE
Think of the Define stage as the moment where you take your understanding of your clients wants and needs and transform it into a clear goal.

You pick out the most important problems your users are facing. Then, you write one simple sentence: a problem statement for our client.

This will explain exactly who is INVOLVED, what kind of way we will problem-solve (VERB) and what result we want (EVENT).
